## Overview

Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) is a vitamin-like compound that plays a critical role in the mitochondrial electron transport chain, where it facilitates ATP (energy) production. It exists in two forms: **ubiquinone** (the oxidized form) and **ubiquinol** (the reduced, more bioavailable form). Given that mitochondrial dysfunction is a well-documented feature of ME/CFS, CoQ10 supplementation is of significant interest to the community as a potential way to support cellular energy production.
