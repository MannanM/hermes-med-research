# Plain Language Summary: Dynamic Redox-Active Self-Assembled Monolayers for Perovskite Solar Cells

## Overview
This study developed a new type of hole-transport layer for tin-lead perovskite solar cells using ferrocene-based self-assembled monolayers. The approach improved energy level alignment and prevented tin oxidation, achieving 23.8% power conversion efficiency with excellent stability.
