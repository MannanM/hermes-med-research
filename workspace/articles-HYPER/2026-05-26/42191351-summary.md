# Plain Language Summary: Coordination Tuning in Na3V2(PO4)3 for Aqueous Sodium-Ion Batteries

## Overview
This materials science study developed a strategy to improve the performance of a material called Na3V2(PO4)3 (NVP) used in aqueous sodium-ion batteries. By substituting zirconium and tungsten atoms into the crystal structure, the researchers stabilized the material and achieved excellent cycling stability over 1000 charge-discharge cycles.
