# Plain Language Summary: Zwitterionic-Enabled Electrochemical Detection of Nucleic Acid Amplification

## Overview
This analytical chemistry study developed a new method for stable, one-pot detection of amplified bacterial DNA using electrochemical sensors. The researchers used a zwitterionic polymer coating to prevent signal instability caused by surface fouling during the reaction.
