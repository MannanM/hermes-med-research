# Plain Language Summary: Trastuzumab Deruxtecan as Neoadjuvant Therapy for HER2-Mutant NSCLC

> **Original Study:** [PMID 42185382](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42185382/) | **Published:** 2026-05-26
> **Local Resources:** [Metadata](./42185382.md) | [Full Text](./42185382-full.txt)

## Overview
This case report describes a patient with operable HER2-mutant lung cancer who received a targeted antibody-drug conjugate (trastuzumab deruxtecan, T-DXd) before surgery. Whole-genome sequencing showed that T-DXd eliminated tumor clones with genome instability and increased immune cell (CD8+ T cell) infiltration into the tumor.
