## Overview
This study investigated tin perovskite solar cells as an alternative to lead-based solar technology. The researchers analyzed energy levels, defects, and charge-transfer dynamics at the interfaces between layers in the solar cell to identify bottlenecks limiting efficiency. This is a materials science/physics study with no medical or connective tissue relevance.

## Key Findings
- This study is about solar cell technology and has no direct relevance to hypermobility disorders or patient health.
